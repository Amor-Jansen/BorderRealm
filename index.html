BorderRealm

The game is a basic 2D scroller game.
There are enimies you will need to attack.
Difficulty can be set by using a scroller bar at the bottom of the page.
All stats will be shown at the top left of the screen.

Controls


The up arrow will move your character up.
The down arrow will move your character down.
The space bar will shoot the fireball/laser.

Mobile controls


Swipe up to go up.
Swipe down to go down.
Tap to shoot fireball/laser.
